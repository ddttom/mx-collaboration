# MX-Card Scenarios

## Venues and Restaurants

Recipes. Tables in a restaurant.

As you enter a premise with a beacon that is unknown to your app, and the identity layer allows notifications, the app will ask if you want to enable the MX-card. If you say yes, the identity layer searches your data for a matching set; if not, it downloads the default data from the venue. The user can adjust it to add inheritance or change options.

This way, a museum can offer a default route through the premises. Or a menu of routes.

If the venue charges, then a share of the proceeds goes to the MX-card hosting site.

## Device Integration

A card QR code on a smartwatch, etc., combined with the device identity layer on the phone or laptop, enables the card to install or configure the connection.

## Government and Identity

Governments can create identity layers for individuals, such as social security numbers. Or age verification certificates, which can be added to the individual identity layer with a permission guard.

## Permission Guards

Permission guards can be added to any facet of the identity layer. AI agents are not told why access to a facet is denied. The user can adjust permissions at will, and agents may not even know if an identity layer exists.

## Portability

Identity layers must be portable. Signed secure transfer to a backup. Or to an owner's new phone or laptop. Identity layers can be globally deleted by a signed application.
