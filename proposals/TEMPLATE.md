# Proposal Title

**Author:** [Your Name]
**Status:** Draft / Review / Accepted
**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

## Background

Provide context for this proposal. What triggered this idea? Link to any relevant background material or discussions.

## Problem

Describe the problem or opportunity this proposal addresses. What is the current state and why is it insufficient?

## Proposed Solution

Detail your proposed solution. Be as specific as possible.

- What changes are needed?
- How will this work?
- Are there any diagrams or mockups?

## Risks and Downsides

What are the potential negative impacts or risks associated with this change? How can they be mitigated?

## Alternatives Considered

What other options did you consider? Why were they rejected?

## Open Questions

List any unresolved questions that need discussion.
